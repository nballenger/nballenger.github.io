Things I have to come to terms with about parenting.

  1. At some point you will have to explain that there is suffering in the world that we see and do not act to alleviate.
  2. You will do things that negatively impact your children for the rest of their lives.
  3. So that they may grow and change, you will let your children experience pain you could have protected them from.
  4. You will not know in advance when these moments will occur.
  5. You will not know the effects of some of your choices until years later, if ever.
